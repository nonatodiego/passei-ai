# Passei AI - Vision 1.0

Passei AI is a daily study copilot. It turns a candidate's available time, plan, study evidence and performance into a clear next action.

The experience is journey-led: the user should open **Hoje**, understand what matters now and begin without negotiating with a complex dashboard. The Study Engine owns future prioritization; product modules contribute typed facts and events.

## Product pillars

- Direction before data entry.
- Execution before reporting.
- Evidence before recommendation.
- Calm, clear and accessible interaction.
- Progressive evolution from local mocks to API-backed intelligence.
